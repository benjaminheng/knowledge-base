title: "Pickling"
---

Basic brine:

- 250g water
- 250g distilled white vinegar
- 20g salt
- 20g sugar

## Batch 1 -- Cucumbers, jalapenos

- Romilino cucumbers, sliced lengthwise
- Jalapeno peppers, slice lengthwise, deseeded
- Peppercorns
- 4 garlic cloves
- Brine:
    - 250g water
    - 250g distilled white vinegar
    - 20g salt
    - 20g sugar
