## Greetings

| English | Malay | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Good morning | Selamat pagi | |
| Good afternoon | Selamat tengah hari | Tengah means middle, hari means day. |
| Good evening | Selamat petang | |

Note that selamat is often pronounced as *sla-mat* instead of *se-la-mat*.
