---
title: "Garlic butter"
type: recipe
ingredients:
    - 5 parts butter
    - 1 part garlic
    - salt
    - black pepper
    - (optional) herbs such as thyme or dill
---

## Directions

- Finely mince [garlic].
- Mix [butter] and [garlic].
- Add [salt] and [black pepper] to taste.

100g of butter to 20g of garlic will yield enough to last several weeks.
